# Data Sources

## 1. FEMA Disaster Declarations Summaries

- **File:** `DisasterDeclarationsSummaries.csv`
- **Source:** OpenFEMA, Federal Emergency Management Agency
- **URL:** https://www.fema.gov/openfema-data-page/disaster-declarations-summaries-v2
- **Date accessed:** March 2026
- **Description:** All federally declared disasters in the United States from 1953 to present, including disaster type, state, declaration date, and which assistance programs were activated.
- **Usage restrictions:** Public domain. Citation requested per OpenFEMA Terms and Conditions.

## 2. Economic Damage from Natural Disasters

- **File:** `economic-damage-from-natural-disasters.csv`
- **Source:** Our World in Data, adapted from EM-DAT (Centre for Research on the Epidemiology of Disasters, UCLouvain)
- **URL:** https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/economic-damage-from-natural-disasters
- **Date accessed:** March 2026
- **Description:** Global economic damage by natural disaster type by year, from 1900 to present. Values in current US dollars.
- **Usage restrictions:** Open access under Creative Commons BY license (Our World in Data). Original EM-DAT data available for non-commercial use.

## 3. FEMA Hazard Mitigation Assistance Projects

- **File:** `HazardMitigationAssistanceProjects.csv`
- **Source:** OpenFEMA, Federal Emergency Management Agency
- **URL:** https://www.fema.gov/openfema-data-page/hazard-mitigation-assistance-projects-v4
- **Date accessed:** March 2026
- **Description:** Project-level data on FEMA hazard mitigation grants, including project type, status, state, fiscal year, and federal funding obligated.
- **Usage restrictions:** Public domain. Citation requested per OpenFEMA Terms and Conditions.

## 4. World Risk Index

- **File:** `world_risk_index.csv`
- **Source:** World Risk Report / Bündnis Entwicklung Hilft & Ruhr University Bochum (via Kaggle)
- **URL:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/tr1gg3rtrash/global-disaster-risk-index-time-series-dataset
- **Date accessed:** March 2026
- **Description:** Country-level disaster risk scores from 2011 to 2021, including exposure, vulnerability, susceptibility, coping capacity, and adaptive capacity.
- **Usage restrictions:** Available for educational and non-commercial use.
